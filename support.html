Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I restore my credits?

Tap the “Restore Purchases” button in Settings. This syncs with Apple/Google. Works across devices with the same account.

2. Why is the generated image blurry or incorrect?

AI performs best with clear, well-lit product photos on neutral backgrounds. Try:

  • High-resolution images (min. 1024px)
  • Removing complex backgrounds
  • Using descriptive prompts
